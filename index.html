Important Information About Soft Washing

‚úì Benefits of Professional Soft Washing

Removes harmful algae, mold, and mildew without damaging delicate surfaces or voiding manufacturer warranties. Eliminates black algae streaks on roofs that reduce curb appeal and shingle lifespan. Safely cleans vinyl siding, stucco, painted surfaces, and wood without high-pressure damage. Uses biodegradable solutions that kill organic growth at the root for longer-lasting results (3-5 times longer than pressure washing). Prevents premature roof replacement by extending shingle life 5-10 years. Restores original appearance and color to exterior surfaces. Protects landscaping with plant-safe cleaning solutions. Increases property value and curb appeal significantly. More effective and safer than pressure washing for most exterior surfaces.

‚ö† Soft Washing Safety Warnings

Never use high pressure on roofs (causes granule loss and shingle damage). Avoid harsh chemicals on painted surfaces or decorative finishes (may cause discoloration). Always protect landscaping before chemical application (plants are sensitive to cleaning solutions). Don't let cleaning solutions dry on surfaces (causes streaking and residue buildup). Never soft wash during windy conditions (overspray can damage plants and adjacent surfaces). Avoid extremely hot or cold temperatures (affects chemical effectiveness). Always verify contractor insurance and certifications before allowing roof work (you're liable for uninsured accidents). Ensure proper chemical dilution ratios (too strong damages surfaces, too weak is ineffective). Keep pets and children away from treated areas until completely rinsed and dried.

‚Ñπ Soft Washing Frequency Recommendations

Myrtle Beach roofs typically need soft washing every 2-3 years due to coastal humidity and algae growth. House exteriors (siding) should be soft washed annually in coastal environments to prevent mold and mildew buildup. North-facing surfaces and shaded areas require more frequent cleaning (every 12-18 months) due to limited sun exposure promoting organic growth. Stucco homes may need soft washing every 1-2 years depending on exposure to elements. Spring (March-May) is optimal for soft washing‚Äîkills algae spores before summer growth season. Fall cleaning (September-November) prepares homes for winter and removes summer buildup. Properties near the ocean may need more frequent cleaning due to salt air accelerating algae growth. Regular soft washing prevents deep staining that becomes harder to remove over time.
